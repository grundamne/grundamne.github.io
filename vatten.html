Vatten - Water
Every dish is carefully crafted, using the essence of the freshwater fountains and rivers of the land of Sweden. This is sure to impart smooth and soothing flavours, while still delivering a satisfying climax of powerful flavours for the dishes of our guests. This is a statement of the beauty of water in Sweden.
