Välkommen til Grundämne!

Grundämne was founded with immense passion. At Grundämne, we are indebted to give our clients an unforgettable experience. Through dining, we showcase our love of food and share the modern cuisine of Sweden. These dishes envoke the true qualities nature has to offer and are paired with exquisite wines all to entreat you with a masterful and fulfilling culinary journey through this Scandinavian wonderland.

Cuisine

Grundämne serves cuisine unparallel with any other restaurant in the city of Göteborg. Grundämne specializes in Modern Swedish Cuisine, using fresh ingredients native to the region. We also borrow influence from British, French and Italian Cuisine. Learn more about the menu by clicking here
